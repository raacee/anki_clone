Our Project

As part of our curriculum at ESILV, we embarked on developing an application inspired by Anki, a popular flashcard-based revision app. Our project, developed in Node/Angular, aims to replicate and enhance some of the key features of Anki to create an enriched and interactive learning experience.

Architecture and Technologies

  • Front-end: Developed using Angular and Bootstrap, offering a responsive and intuitive user interface.
  • Back-end: Node.js, providing a solid foundation for server-side operations.
  • Database: Postgres, for efficient and secure data management.
  • APIs: Utilizing APIs to query the database and facilitate communication between the front-end and back-end.

Organization and Methodology

To effectively carry out our project, we adopted the Scrum methodology, leveraging Jira for task organization and project management. This approach enabled efficient collaboration within the team and steady progress of the project.

Skills and Learning

This project has allowed us to enhance our skills in several key areas:

  • Web/App Development: Deepening our knowledge in Angular, Bootstrap, and Node.
  • Software Engineering: Applying principles of software design and architecture.
  • Teamwork and Organization: Gaining practical experience in team collaboration within an agile framework.

We are proud of our accomplishments and hope that our application will bring significant added value to those looking to enhance their learning process.
